CAD Work

This section highlights a range of CAD projects demonstrating creative mechanical design, practical engineering solutions, and technical proficiency in SolidWorks and Fusion 360. These projects span academic research, client products, and real-world prototyping.
Large-Scale 3D Printed Wooden House Prototype
Designed and built a large-format 3D printer to explore the feasibility of printing full-scale wooden structures. Modeled all components in SolidWorks and validated structural elements through physical testing and iteration.
Continuous Casting Machine R&D
Developed and optimized a moldless casting system using mechanical design and 3D modeling. Applied SolidWorks to refine moving components and integrated control systems to automate the process.
HP Sustainable Packaging Cover
Engineered a sustainable packaging solution for HP in collaboration with a research team. Designed models in Fusion 360 and focused on material reduction, strength-to-weight performance, and production feasibility.
Spinner Template Painting Jig
Created a precise jig to streamline the painting process of spinner blades. Modeled and tested multiple iterations to ensure compatibility with various spinner sizes and consistent output.
Fishing Lure Prototypes
Designed and modified several fishing lure prototypes using SolidWorks and Fusion 360. Improvements focused on action tuning, manufacturability, and tooling compatibility.
